Frequently Asked Questions

What are widgets and how do they work?

Widgets are specialized tools within our applications that provide advanced diagnostics and repair capabilities. While our base applications are free and include basic features, premium widgets unlock the full power of our tools. After subscribing, you can download and use these widgets directly within the application interface.

How do I access premium widgets after subscribing?

Accessing premium features is simple. After signing up with your email and password, you'll receive a confirmation email. Then, download the software from our Downloads page, open it, and log in using the same email and password you registered with. The system will automatically recognize your premium status, and all premium features will be immediately available to you without any additional steps.

Does my subscription cover all tools in the suite?

Yes, each subscription provides access to premium widgets across our entire suite of tools. You can use any of our tools (FixMyWindows, FixMyMac, FixMyData, etc.) on one device with a single subscription. The business plan allows usage across multiple devices.

What happens if my subscription expires?

If your subscription expires, you'll maintain access to the base application, but premium widgets will no longer function until you renew your subscription. Any reports or data you've generated using the widgets will still be accessible, but you won't be able to run new premium diagnostics or repairs until your subscription is renewed.

Can I transfer my subscription to another computer?

Yes, your subscription is tied to your account, not to a specific device. You can install the application on multiple computers (up to the limit specified in your plan) and sign in with your account credentials to access your premium widgets on each device.

Are subscriptions automatically renewed?

Yes, for your convenience, subscriptions automatically renew at the end of your subscription period. You can manage your subscription settings, including auto-renewal, through your account dashboard. We'll send you a reminder email before each renewal to ensure you're aware of the upcoming charge.
