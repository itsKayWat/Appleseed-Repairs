Shipping Instructions

Follow these steps to safely package and ship your device

Packaging Your Device

  • Use original packaging if available
  • Wrap device in bubble wrap (minimum 2 inches thick)
  • Place in sturdy cardboard box
  • Fill empty spaces with packing material
  • Seal all edges with packing tape

Required Documentation

  • Print and include repair authorization form
  • Add detailed description of issues
  • Include your contact information
  • Note any specific repair requests
  • Add your repair quote reference number

Shipping Your Device

  • Use provided shipping label
  • Ship via UPS, FedEx, or USPS
  • Get tracking number
  • Insure package for full value
  • Keep shipping receipt

Important: Please remove any passwords or activation locks from your device before shipping. Back up your data as repair process may require system reset.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our mail-in repair service

How long does the repair process take?

Most repairs are completed within 24-48 hours after we receive your device. Shipping typically takes 2-3 business days each way, so the total process usually takes 5-7 business days.

What if my device can't be repaired?

If we can't repair your device, you won't be charged for the repair attempt. You'll only need to pay return shipping. We'll provide detailed information about the issue and recommend alternative solutions.

Is my data safe during repair?

We take data security seriously. However, we recommend backing up your data before sending your device, as some repairs may require system resets. All our technicians follow strict privacy protocols.

What warranty do you offer?

All our repairs come with a 90-day warranty covering both parts and labor. If you experience any issues related to our repair within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.
