Device Preparation

Screen Removal

Apply heat to the edges of the phone for about 1 minute to soften the adhesive. Use the suction handle to create a small gap.

Heat Gun
3

Battery Disconnection

Once the screen is lifted, immediately locate and disconnect the battery connector.

Spudger
4

Display Cable Disconnection

Carefully disconnect the display cables and remove the metal shield covering them.

Y000 Screwdriver
5

Battery Removal

Remove the battery adhesive strips and carefully lift out the battery.

Adhesive Remover
6

Camera System Removal

Remove the camera shield and disconnect the camera system connectors.

Y000 Screwdriver
7

Logic Board Removal

Remove all visible screws and carefully disconnect all connectors from the logic board.

Multiple Screwdrivers
8

Reassembly Process

Carefully reassemble all components in reverse order, ensuring proper connection of all cables.

⚠️ Important Warnings

Battery Safety:

Never puncture or bend the battery. If swelling occurs, immediately discontinue repair.

Screen Handling:

OLED screens are extremely fragile. Handle edges with care.

Screw Management:

Keep track of all screws. Different lengths can damage components if mixed up.

Water Damage:

Check liquid indicators before proceeding with any repair.

Face ID Components:

Handle front sensor assembly with extreme care to maintain Face ID functionality.

Device Specifications

  • Dimensions: 159.9 × 76.7 × 8.25 mm
  • Weight: 221 g
  • Display: 6.7-inch Super Retina XDR OLED
  • Resolution: 2796 × 1290 pixels (460 ppi)
  • Processor: A17 Pro chip
  • RAM: 8 GB
  • Battery: 4422 mAh
  • Water Resistance: IP68 (maximum depth of 6 meters up to 30 minutes)

Important Safety Notice

Before beginning any repair:

  • Power down the device completely
  • Disconnect the battery as soon as possible
  • Wear appropriate safety gear (ESD strap, eye protection)
  • Keep track of all screws and components
  • Work in a clean, well-lit environment

Common Issues & Solutions

Face ID Not Working

Common causes:

  • Damaged or disconnected front sensor assembly
  • Improper screen replacement
  • Software calibration issues

Solution: Verify all front sensor connections and ensure proper calibration.

Camera Malfunction

Possible issues:

  • Loose camera connectors
  • Damaged flex cables
  • Improper camera alignment

Solution: Check all camera connections and verify proper mounting.
